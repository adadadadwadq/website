Privacy Policy – Roblox Watcher & Exploit Watcher

Effective Date: December 8, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Roblox Watcher and Exploit Watcher handle and store information while operating as Discord moderation tools. By using the bots, you consent to these practices. If you do not agree, you must remove the bots from your server.

1. Information We Collect

  • Server IDs and server names
  • Channel IDs for autoban notifications
  • Discord IDs, usernames, nicknames, timestamps
  • Banlist and whitelist records
  • Autoban and moderation settings
  • The bot’s command prefix
  • Logs of user deletion requests
  • Information provided directly by Discord through its API — no external tracking, fingerprinting, or metadata collection occurs.

We do NOT collect: IP addresses, device identifiers, cookies, messages, or any data outside of what Discord provides through its API.

2. Purpose of Data

  • Maintain server configurations
  • Enforce automated moderation actions
  • Track whitelist and banlist entries
  • Generate moderation logs and notifications

Data is never sold, shared for advertising, or profiled externally.

3. Data Retention

All data collected by the bots is retained indefinitely to protect communities, enforce bans, and prevent abuse. Users may submit a one-time /privacy request, but data will generally be retained permanently as part of our operational procedures.

Only the bot owner and authorized moderators have access to stored data.

4. Appeal Information

Users may appeal moderation decisions via the official support Discord server. Certain bans may be unappealable unless innocence is proven.

5. Security

Databases are secured using standard practices. No system is completely infallible, but precautions are in place to prevent unauthorized access.

6. Legal Basis

Retention of data is justified under GDPR “legitimate interest” to protect Discord communities. Data is generally retained permanently to enforce moderation and prevent abuse.

7. Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect operational changes, security improvements, or compliance requirements. Continued use after updates indicates acceptance.

8. Contact

For questions or concerns, contact support via the official support Discord server.
