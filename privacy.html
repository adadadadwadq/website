Privacy Policy – Roblox Watcher & Exploit Watcher

Effective Date: December 8, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Roblox Watcher and Exploit Watcher handle and store information while operating as Discord moderation tools. By using the bots, you consent to these practices.

1. Information We Collect

  • Discord IDs, usernames, and nicknames
  • Server IDs and names
  • Channel IDs for notifications
  • Timestamps of moderation actions
  • Banlist and whitelist records
  • Autoban and moderation settings
  • Bot command prefix
  • User deletion request logs
  • Search terms acceptance logs
  • Slash command registration logs

2. Purpose of Data

  • Maintain server configurations and settings
  • Enforce moderation actions
  • Track banlist and whitelist entries
  • Generate logs for transparency and accountability

3. Data Retention

All data is retained indefinitely under legitimate interest to protect communities, enforce moderation, and prevent abuse. Users may submit a one-time /privacy request for deletion, but data may be retained if necessary for moderation.

Access is limited to the bot owner and authorized moderators.

4. Data Sharing

Data is never sold or shared with third parties, except if legally required. All information is used exclusively for bot functionality and moderation.

5. Security

Reasonable security measures are in place to protect stored data. While precautions are taken, absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

6. User Rights & Appeals

Users may appeal moderation actions via the official support Discord. Final decisions are made by the bot owner and authorized moderators.

7. Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect operational, security, or legal changes. Continued use indicates acceptance of updates.

8. Contact

For questions or concerns, contact the bot owner via the official support Discord.
