Privacy Policy – Roblox Watcher & Exploit Watcher

This Privacy Policy explains how Roblox Watcher and Exploit Watcher collect, store, and process information while operating as Discord moderation tools. By using the Bots, you agree to the practices explained below. If you do not agree, you must remove the Bots from your server.

Effective Date: October 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect & Store

The Bots only collect information required to operate moderation features. This includes:

  • Server Data: Server IDs, server names, and configuration settings.
  • Channel Data: Channel IDs for autoban/alert features.
  • User Data: User IDs, usernames, action timestamps.
  • Enforcement Data: Banlists, whitelists, logs, and identifiers tied to moderation enforcement.

Only minimal data necessary for functionality is stored.

2. Purpose of Data

All collected data is used strictly for moderation purposes, such as:

  • Maintaining server configuration settings.
  • Running automated moderation actions.
  • Storing banlist and whitelist entries.
  • Generating moderation logs and alerts.

Data is never sold or used for advertising. It is stored only to operate Roblox Watcher and Exploit Watcher correctly.

Banlist and enforcement data is retained permanently to maintain accurate moderation history and prevent repeated abuse. This is considered a legitimate interest under GDPR and similar global standards.

3. Data Deletion & Legitimate Interest

Users may request information about what data is stored about them. However, requests for deletion may be declined if the data is required for:

  • Security and abuse prevention
  • Maintaining accurate ban history
  • Protecting communities moderated by the Bots

Because enforcement data is essential to the integrity of the Bots, banlist entries are generally not removed unless an appeal is approved. This complies with GDPR’s legitimate interest basis.

4. Security Information

  • We take precautions to ensure our systems and database remain secure.
  • No system is completely immune to vulnerabilities, but reasonable protections are in place.

5. Appeal Information

  • If you believe your ID was added to the banlist by mistake, you may submit an appeal through the official Roblox Watcher Discord server.
  • Successful appeals may result in removal of your ID. Inappropriate experience bans are unappealable unless proven innocent.
  • Unsuccessful appeals will not result in removal of stored data.

6. Updates to This Privacy Policy

  • This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect operational, security, or compliance changes.
  • Continued use of the Bots means you accept the updated version.
