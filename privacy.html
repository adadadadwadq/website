Roblox Watcher & Exploit Watcher Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy governs your use of Roblox Watcher and Exploit Watcher, Discord moderation bots designed to protect servers and enforce safe community standards. By installing or using these bots, you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, you must remove the bots from your server.

Last Updated: 01/10/25

1. Information We Collect

  • Discord User IDs: Numeric IDs may be stored for moderation purposes.
  • Usernames: Temporarily accessed during moderation checks or reports, but not stored permanently.
  • Server Information: Basic server data, such as server ID and member list, may be accessed to perform moderation actions.
  • Logs: Activity related to bot actions may be logged internally by the bot for functionality and security purposes.

2. How We Use the Information

  • To detect and manage users who violate Roblox Community Standards.
  • To generate moderation reports and notifications.
  • To maintain the security and proper operation of the bots.

3. Data Storage

  • All necessary data is stored securely by the bot and accessible only by the bot owner.
  • No personal messages, server content, or other sensitive data are stored.
  • Data is retained only as long as required for moderation purposes.

4. Data Sharing

  • Roblox Watcher and Exploit Watcher do not sell, share, or distribute collected data to third parties.
  • Data is used strictly for the functionality and security of the bots.

5. Security

  • The bot owner takes reasonable measures to protect stored data.
  • Absolute security cannot be guaranteed, but safeguards are implemented to prevent unauthorized access.

6. User Rights

  • If you believe your Discord ID has been flagged or affected in error, you may contact the bot owner via the official Roblox Watcher Discord server.

7. Changes to This Policy

  • This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect new practices, features, or compliance requirements.
  • Significant changes will be communicated via bot updates, documentation, or the official Discord server.
  • Continued use of the bots after updates constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
