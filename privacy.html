This Privacy Policy explains how Roblox Watcher and Exploit Watcher handle and store information while operating as Discord moderation tools. By using the bots, you acknowledge and consent to the practices outlined here. If you do not agree, you must remove the bots from your server.

Effective Date: October 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect & Store

To provide moderation features, the Bots collect and store limited Discord-related information, including:

  • Server Data: Server IDs and server names.
  • Channel Data: Channel IDs configured for autoban notifications.
  • User Data: User IDs, usernames, and timestamps of actions.
  • Enforcement Data: Records of bans, banlists, and whitelists that may contain user and server identifiers.

2. Purpose Of Data

The collected information is used exclusively for moderation-related functions, including:

  • Maintaining configuration records for servers.
  • Enforcing automated moderation actions.
  • Tracking whitelist and banlist entries.
  • Generating necessary moderation logs and notifications.

Information is never sold or shared externally and is not used for advertising or profiling. Its sole purpose is to enable the functionality of the Bots and support server moderation.

All data mentioned above is permanently stored within our database.

3. Your Data

  • Stored data is not sold, traded, or shared with outside organizations.
  • Banlist information is used only for the sole purpose of Roblox Watcher and Exploit Watcher.
  • In rare cases, data may be shared if legally required (e.g., responding to lawful requests).

4. Security Information

  • We take precautions in ensuring our database is secure.
  • Although protections are in place, no system can be considered completely infallible.

5. Appeal Information

  • If you believe your account has been added to the banlist by mistake, you may submit an appeal request through the official Roblox Watcher Discord server.
  • Successful appeals may result in the removal of your ID from the stored banlists. Note: innapropiate experience bans are unappealable unless proven innocent.

6. Updates to This Privacy Policy

  • This Privacy Policy may be revised to reflect changes in how the bots operate, security improvements, or compliance requirements. Updates may also be made as needed to reflect changes in policy, features, or compliance requirements. Continued use of the bots after such updates means you accept the revised Privacy Policy.
